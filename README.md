# Office Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive data analytics project analyzing workforce demographics, compensation structures, and organizational growth trends using Power BI.

## 📊 Project Overview

This project provides an in-depth analysis of organizational workforce data, examining employee demographics, compensation equity, recruitment trends, and operational structure. The analysis leverages Power BI to visualize and interpret data patterns, enabling data-driven insights for strategic decision-making.

## 📈 Analysis Performed

**Tool Used**: Power BI

### 1. Workforce Overview & Demographics
- **Total Headcount**: 161 employees
- **Age Distribution**: Predominantly younger workforce concentrated in the 25–35 age range
- **Gender Balance**: 
  - Female employees: 51.16%
  - Male employees: 48.84%
  - Nearly equal gender distribution

### 2. Compensation Analysis
- **Average Salary**: $54.23K
- **Salary Range**: $29K – $85K (spread of $56K)
- **Gender Pay Equity**:
  - Female average salary: $55.37K
  - Male average salary: $52.86K
  - Pay gap: ~4.7% (indicates balanced compensation practices)

### 3. Growth & Recruitment Trends
- **Early Phase (2018–2019)**: Slow growth from 1 to 21 employees
- **Expansion Phase (2020–2023)**: 
  - Rapid acceleration with 160% growth
  - Growth from 62 employees (early 2020) to 161 employees (late 2023)
  - Indicates significant business scale-up or market expansion

### 4. Operational Structure
- **Top 3 Job Titles** (37% of headcount):
  - Packaging Associate: 22 employees
  - Production Operator: 20 employees
  - Sales Representative: 18 employees
- **Specialized Roles** (smallest portion):
  - Marketing Specialists: 10 employees
  - Managers: 10 employees

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

🎯 **Retention Strategy**
- Focus on long-term career pathing for employees in the 25–35 age bracket
- Develop career progression programs to retain "growth phase" talent

🎯 **Operational Scaling**
- Conduct efficiency audits in production departments (Packaging/Production roles dominate)
- Optimize workflows to maximize ROI in high-headcount areas
